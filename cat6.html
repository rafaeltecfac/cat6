Cat6.US

We are in the communications business!

Structured Cabling is one of those things that may not be on your radar when planning your new office space or when reworking your current location. Let us help you map out the most effective way to accomplish all of your cabling needs.

Structured cabling greatly simplifies the installation of a cable infrastructure that supports a wide variety of voice and data communications equipment. Whether installing a coaxial outlet in a home or wiring a skyscraper, the components are all made to specific standards for electrical transmission, resistance, cable lengths, connectors and cable fabrication.

All of your connectivity needs are met painlessly!

We offer Network Cabling for PC’s, legacy phones, VoIP telephones, Cameras, printers, copiers, AP’s and other devices etc. Whatever you need to connect to your local area network or your wide area network. While we specialize in Cat6 cabling we offer a variety of cable needs to include Cat5, Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6A, coaxial, pre-terminated fiber cabling and more. We can install new structured cabling. We can evaluate your existing cabling, test existing segments for Cat5/5e/6/6A validation, and apply labels to patch panels, jacks and cables.
